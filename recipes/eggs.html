Scrambled Eggs

Description

A simple egg dish cooked in a pan until soft and set, commonly served for breakfast or as a quick meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp butter or oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper (optional)

Steps

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl and beat lightly.
  2. Heat butter or oil in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour in eggs and stir gently until cooked.
  4. Season with salt and pepper. Serve warm.
