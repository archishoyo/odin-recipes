Simple Pancakes

Description

A basic batter cooked on a flat pan to make soft, round pancakes, often served for breakfast or dessert.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp oil or butter

Steps

  1. Mix flour, sugar, and baking powder in a bowl.
  2. Add milk, egg, and oil. Mix until smooth.
  3. Heat a pan over medium heat.
  4. Pour batter and cook until bubbles form, then flip.
  5. Cook until golden and serve.
